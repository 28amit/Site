ABOUT US

A 2-year roller coaster started from Pune which we had undergone to pursue a dream, was reaching its next step. We had built a sound business and were a strong team who had managed to overcome various challenges. We were gearing up to launch in 3 more cities.

And then comes the villain in our story, the word COVID storms its way into our lives, followed by the term lockdown. Further, a notice saying all offices & studios must be shut. The word “community” on which the essence of our business was based on, had become the biggest threat.

As usual, we took it up as one of that zillionth challenge in our journey. Few minutes to absorb the situation and then turned the hustle mode on till the problems were solved. We launched online classes, trained our partners to go online. It was time to make some tough calls. We had to let go of some members of the team, we left our offices, returned the coffee machine and were almost back to Day 1 and back to the basics to ensure we survive.

We are a smaller team now and continue to provide the same quality of skills and services online. Our online services include some exciting courses like Cake baking, Candle Making, Make-up, Spoken English, Tailoring, Cooking and much more at the most cost-efficient price ever.
